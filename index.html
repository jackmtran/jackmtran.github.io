About Me.

Hello! I'm Jack Tran, and I use he/him pronouns. As a dedicated problem solver, I thrive on tackling challenges and honing in on the finer details that drive project success. With extensive experience collaborating within diverse teams, I’m always eager to step in and fill gaps wherever needed to ensure our collective goals are met. While I am a full-stack software engineer, my true passion lies in front-end development. I love creating engaging and dynamic web applications, and I have a solid foundation in technologies such as HTML, JavaScript, Python, React, Flask, Express.js, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. I particularly enjoy leveraging Tailwind CSS to craft visually stunning and responsive designs that enhance user experiences. I am also very well-versed in Figma, which allows me to effectively outline website designs and collaborate with design teams to translate concepts into functional interfaces. My experience with Express.js enables me to build robust server-side applications and APIs, seamlessly integrating them with front-end technologies to create cohesive web solutions. I’m excited about the opportunity to bring innovative ideas to life and contribute to projects that truly make a difference.
